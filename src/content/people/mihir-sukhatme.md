I am a Ph.D. student in Computational and Systems Biology, co-advised by Myriam Heiman and Ila Fiete.

Broadly, I am interested in understanding genetic fates in a neurodegenerative context. I work on modeling genetic trajectories of outcomes such as Huntington's Disease, using various transcriptomic modalities.

Previously, I studied Computational and Systems Biology as an undergraduate at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). During undergrad, I worked under the supervision of Dr. Paivi Pajukanta, where I studied the genetic risk mechanisms underlying obesity-related traits like Type-2 Diabetes (T2D).

Outside the lab, I enjoy playing sports like volleyball and tennis, and you'll almost always see me with headphones on, listening to new music!
